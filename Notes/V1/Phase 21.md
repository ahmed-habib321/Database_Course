## 2Ô∏è‚É£ Association Rules
- Discover relationships between variables in datasets 
- Example: Market Basket Analysis 
  - Rule: `{Bread, Butter} ‚Üí {Milk}` ‚Üí customers who buy bread & butter often buy milk 
- Metrics:
  - Support: Frequency of the itemset 
  - Confidence: Probability of occurrence given the antecedent 
  - Lift: Strength of association beyond random chance

Applications: Retail, e-commerce, recommendation systems

## 3Ô∏è‚É£ Classification
- Predict categorical labels for data based on features 
- Examples of algorithms: Decision Trees, Naive Bayes, k-NN 
- Example: Predict whether a customer will churn (Yes/No) based on usage data

Steps:
1. Train model on historical labeled data 
2. Test model on new data 
3. Evaluate accuracy & refine

## 4Ô∏è‚É£ Clustering
- Group similar data points together (unsupervised learning)
- Does not require labeled data 
- Examples of algorithms: k-Means, DBSCAN, Hierarchical clustering 
- Applications:
  - Customer segmentation 
  - Image recognition 
  - Outlier detection

- Anomaly Detection: Identify unusual patterns (fraud detection, network intrusion)
- Sequential Patterns: Detect sequences in time-series data (e.g., shopping habits)
